DEVELOPMENT PHILOSOPHY

I believe in Deep Ownership. Beyond writing code, I strive to understand the entire delivery chain—from database commit logs and memory management to final binary deployment. I am guided by:

  • Intentional Tooling: I choose stacks based on problem-solving efficiency rather than industry trends. This pragmatic approach ensures the right tool is used for the specific performance requirements of the task.
  • Minimalism: I prioritize low dependency counts to reduce attack surfaces, minimize maintenance debt, and ensure lightning-fast execution—whether in a systems binary or a browser-based UI.
  • Transparency: I value understanding the abstractions I use. My experience with systems-level languages (C, Go, Rust) provides me with a unique perspective on optimizing high-level applications and debugging complex performance bottlenecks.
